                  Fiddle & Pipes was founded by musician Michael Sean O'Barrett, who traveled Ireland with his fiddle collecting the best local recipes. O'Barrett, who learned to cook from his grandmother, envisioned a pub where beer, music, and conversation flowed.

                  He immigrated to the United States in 1897, and made his way to Madison, Wisconsin, where he was reminded of the loughs of home. He decided to settle down, and open his dream pub.

                  Today, Fiddle & Pipes offers live music and O'Barrett's original recipes seven days a week. We hope you'll join us for a pint.
